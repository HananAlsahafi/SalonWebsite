40 Years of Beauty Co Salon and Spa Experience


Welcome Beauty Co to Salon and Spa, where we have been perfecting the art of pampering for over 40 years! Serving the entire Pennsylvania Main Line area and beyond since 1977, we service clients from the Jeddah, Khilas, Ghran, Makkah, Abu Dhabe and Riydh area with Beauty Co in salon services from head to toe.

During the last 40+ years, our salon has seen many changes and expansions. The most recent was the opening of our standalone state-of-the-art Salon and Day Spa. This facility was built from the ground up to provide our clients with the best service possible. We look forward to a wonderful future together providing you the “Ultimate” in your hair, skin, body, waxing and massage needs.

Our success is dependent on the satisfaction of our clients, so we have a different approach to providing you with an entirely pampering and relaxing salon experience. Every new client is treated to a complimentary consultation, where we aim to discover what your needs and goals are of achieving great results. We know that when you feel good about your appearance and presentation, you will achieve self-confidence and a feeling of renewed energy.
